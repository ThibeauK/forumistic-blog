Welcome to My Blog

⎇ with forumistic tendencies


This webpage is a digital outlet for my interest in computational technology and its kinship with graphic design. Utilising hardware hacking tactics to investigate their relationship. It is partially populated with theoretical exploration and explanatory blog posts displaying physical inquiries. These illustrate the creation of a single purpose electronic tool, engineered exclusively to highlight critical technological awareness. I invite the reader to take up a position of fellow researcher. Using #comments-container to expand the narrative. Simultaneously shaping this digital facility.

Platform utilises the chronological ruleset of the blog.
